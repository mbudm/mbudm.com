How Chopsumi works

Chopsumi lets your friends vote on your next event. Simply suggest an activity, some dates and locations. Then invite your friends to vote on the where and when — Chopsumi works out the best option!

  1. Decide what you want to do
  2. Add where and when options
  3. Send invites to your friends
  4. Everyone votes on the options
  5. Chopsumi works out your plan
